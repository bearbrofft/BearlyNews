Bearly News

Hi. Welcome to Bearly News, a personal blog dedicated to logging my personal experiences in all aspects of my life, along with sharing key pieces of knowledge that I have gained along the way. I've always had an interest in sharing the things I love with others, but I'm an old-school guy who would rather show his personality through his writing than sit in front of a camera (at least for the time being).

Although many people may never look at this webpage, I will continue to contribute daily so that I can not only perfect my storytelling, but also hone my web development skills. I'm excited to grow this webpage and get to share with you how my life has developed.
